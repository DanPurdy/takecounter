Controls

Currently set to use a numpad.

To change you need to adjust the key codes  within the defaults section of the scripts.js file.

A simple list of key codes can be found here http://www.webonweboff.com/tips/js/event_key_codes.aspx

Takes

+ / Plus		Take Up
-  / Minus		Take Minus
*  / Asterix		Take Select (prompts user to enter a take number)

Pass's

6 / Six			Pass Up
9 / Nine		Pass Minus
7 / Seven		New Pass (Increments pass and resets take to 1)

Utility

. / Period		Rest Counter (resets take and pass to 1)
4 / Four		Switch between pass & take mode to take mode (allows you to just use takes if pass's aren't required.)
